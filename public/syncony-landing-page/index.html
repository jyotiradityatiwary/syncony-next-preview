open-source, easy to host cloud storage

Freedom of Choice

Choose any hosting provider, or host it yourself, locally or on the cloud. We tried our best to make self hosting easy!

Transparency

You are free to audit our code, or have it audited. The work is all in the open!

Security

Your files are always stored encrypted and the server owner can see nothing! Files are encrypted with your accounts password before being sent off to the server so you do not need to trust the backend.

Privacy

No data collected about app usage, about files stored or anything else by us. Everything stays between you and your provider.

Want to self host?

We got you covered. We tried our best to make self hosting as easy and straight forward as possible.
